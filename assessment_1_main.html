Basic Illness/Conditions

  1. Common cold - Symptoms: Runny nose, Sore Throat, Coughing
  2. Flu - Symptoms: Chills, Fever, Fatigue
  3. Allergies - Symptoms: Reaction to substances; pollen, dust, pets
Severe Illness/Conditions
  1. Athsma - Symptoms: Difficulty breathing, wheezing, coughing
  2. Chickenpox - Symptoms: Itchy rash, red spots, blisters
  3. COVID-19 - Symptoms: Difficulty Breathing, Lost of taste or smell
